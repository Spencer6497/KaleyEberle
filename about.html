About Me

Kaley Eberle was born and raised in Cincinnati, Ohio. Growing up, she participated in many musical ventures such as theatre, choir, band, and her high school acappella group. At the age of 8, she started playing guitar and at 15 started playing piano.

At 12, she started her songwriting journey.
